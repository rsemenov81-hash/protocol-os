### ABSOLUTE NO list
Carprofen or ANY NSAID • ibuprofen (Advil) • naproxen (Aleve) • aspirin •
acetaminophen (Tylenol) • Pepto-Bismol • turmeric/curcumin (until fully healed) •
high-dose fish oil (until stool normal ~1 week)

### Daily monitoring log (60 seconds)
- **Gums:** lift lip — bubble-gum pink = good. Pale/white/grey = EMERGENCY.
- **Stool:** color (black/tarry = bleed), blood, consistency, mucus.
- **Eating / vomiting / energy:** yes-no-normal?

### 🚨 EMERGENCY TRIGGERS — home care ends, she needs a clinic
Pale/white gums • collapse or marked weakness • black tarry stool • repeated
vomiting despite omeprazole • refusing food >24–48h • swollen painful belly •
fresh red blood or bloody jelly mucus in stool • straining repeatedly producing
nothing.
*(If triggered: nearest ER/low-cost clinic; RedRover & Paws 4 A Cure are the
fast-response aid charities.)*

1. **Green-lipped mussel + glucosamine/chondroitin chew** (e.g., Cosequin DS Max
   Strength or GLM-based equivalent, large-dog label dose; loading dose 4–6 wks
   then maintenance). Gentlest on gut — start first.
2. **Fish oil (omega-3):** target **~1,600–2,400 mg combined EPA+DHA/day**
   (check EPA+DHA on label, NOT total fish oil). Start 1 capsule/day with food,
   add a capsule every 3–4 days. Back off if stool softens.

**Lifestyle (free, highest-impact):**

- Traction: rugs/yoga mats on slick floors. Orthopedic/thick foam bed.
- Ramps or steps for car/furniture; raised bowls.
- **Short frequent walks** (2–3 × 10–15 min) > one long walk.
- Warm compress on stiff joints ~10 min in the morning.

**Next medical step when budget allows:** telehealth visit (~$30–60) →
**gabapentin script** (cheap via GoodRx; the best safe analgesic available to
her) + **sucralfate** + Apoquel refill + GI recheck.
